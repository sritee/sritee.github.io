Sridhar Thiagarajan

Hello there! I'm Sridhar, a masters student in the robotics research program at Oregon State University's Collaborative Robotics and Intelligent Systems (CoRIS) Institute. My interests are in Machine Learning and Reinforcement Learning applied to robotics. Apart from sharing my experiences, this website serves as an avenue to blog about some of the research papers/topics I have been reading.

Recently, I interned at Honda Research Institute USA , and worked on reinforcement learning for deformable object manipulation on real robots. Prior to that, I was a Reinforcement Learning and Robotics Intern at Swaayatt Robots, a company working on a self-driving vehicles for Indian Roads. I was also spent time with IIIT Delhi's Machine Learning group, and worked on one-shot imitation learning using egocentric demonstrations. Feel free to contact me if you have any questions, or want to chat about some common technical interests.
